Barcelona Food & Cafés

Breakfast Options Near the Apartment

  • Federal Café (Poble Sec): Modern café with eggs, toast, coffee. Opens 8:00 AM (10 min walk).
  • Tarannà Café: Cozy with pastries, bowls, and espresso drinks. Opens 8:00 AM (8–10 min walk).
  • Cafe Cometa: Light breakfast options, fruit bowls, toast. Opens 8:00 AM (8 min walk).
  • La Roseta (Hotel Brummell): Stylish café, early morning vibe. Opens 7:30 AM (5 min walk).

Dinner Options Nearby

  • Agust Gastrobar: Refined tapas in a relaxed setting. 4 min walk. [agustbarcelona.com]
  • Bodega 1900: Traditional Spanish feel with vermouth and tapas. 3–5 min walk.
  • Cañete: Elegant Catalan, one of the best nearby. 10 min walk. [restaurantcanete.com]
  • Louro: Galician cuisine with great wine. 9 min walk.

Casual Eats & Teen-Friendly Cafés

  • MACERA TallerBar: Burgers, tacos, mocktails. Youthful vibe. 5–6 min walk.
  • La Tasqueta de Blai: Pintxos street — try a bit of everything! Carrer de Blai, ~10 min walk.
  • Pizza Circus: Giant slices, casual fun. 2 min walk.
  • Ramen-ya Hiro: Best ramen in the city. 10–12 min walk. Go early!
  • Taco Alto (Raval): Build-your-own tacos. 9–10 min walk.
  • Flax & Kale Street: Healthy bowls, smoothies. Instagrammable. 10 min walk.

Bakeries & Cafés Teens Will Love

  • Chök – The Chocolate Kitchen: Donuts, cronuts, chocolate heaven. 10 min walk.
  • Brunch & Cake: Pancakes, smoothie bowls. Very popular. 10–12 min walk.
  • La Donuteria: Donuts with fun flavours. Close by in Poble Sec.
  • Pastisseria Escribà (Gran Via): Historic, whimsical pastries. 10 min walk.
  • Satan's Coffee Corner: Edgy minimalist café with great coffee. Further away but worth a stop if in Eixample.
