Barcelona Itinerary – July 2–3, 2025

✨ Optional Attractions if Time

  • Casa Batlló
    📍 Passeig de Gràcia, 43
    Why go: Gaudí’s immersive masterpiece with interactive exhibits.
    Best fit: Afternoon of 1 July after lunch.
    🎟 Entry ~€35; includes audio guide. Closest metro: Passeig de Gràcia (L2/L3/L4).
  • Casa Milà (La Pedrera)
    📍 Passeig de Gràcia, 92
    Why go: Rooftop chimneys and dreamlike interiors.
    Best fit: Combine with Casa Batlló.
    🎟 Entry ~€28. Metro: Diagonal (L3/L5).
  • Picasso Museum
    📍 Carrer de Montcada, 15-23
    Why go: Picasso’s early works in a gothic setting.
    Best fit: After Gothic Quarter walk on 1 July.
    🎟 Entry ~€12. Metro: Jaume I (L4).
  • Palau de la Música Catalana
    📍 C/ Palau de la Música, 4-6
    Why go: Ornate modernist concert hall.
    Best fit: Late morning or afternoon on 2 July.
    🎟 Entry ~€16 (guided tour). Metro: Urquinaona (L1/L4).
  • El Born Cultural Centre
    📍 Plaça Comercial, 12
    Why go: Archaeological site of medieval streets.
    Best fit: Combine with Gothic Quarter or Picasso Museum.
    🎟 Free entry. Metro: Jaume I (L4).
  • Tibidabo Hill & Amusement Park
    📍 Pl. del Tibidabo, 3-4
    Why go: Panoramic city views and vintage rides.
    Best fit: Only if skipping Montjuïc or beach.
    🎟 Park entry €35. Take Tramvia Blau + funicular or Tibibus.
  • Gaudí Experience (4D)
    📍 C/ Larrard, 41 (near Park Güell)
    Why go: Interactive VR and 4D on Gaudí’s world.
    Best fit: Pair with Sagrada Família or afternoon on 1 July.
    🎟 Entry ~€9. Metro: Lesseps (L3).
  • Chocolate Museum
    📍 Carrer del Comerç, 36
    Why go: Chocolate sculptures & history.
    Best fit: Combine with El Born on 1 July.
    🎟 Entry ~€6. Includes a bar of chocolate! Metro: Arc de Triomf (L1).
