Hotel Details

Stay Together Barcelona Apartments

Our spacious apartment is perfect for families and groups traveling together, and its central location makes it an ideal base for exploring Barcelona on your own.

Our reception desk is in the same building, and we'll be there to warmly welcome you and assist you between 10 am and 6 pm. For late arrivals, we'll provide you with easy self-check-in instructions.

To ensure a smooth check-in and a comfortable stay, please provide us with the following information:

  • Your estimated arrival time and flight number, and whether you'll be arriving together. Do you need any assistance with transportation, such as a private taxi or public transport suggestions?
  • Your check-out time on the day of departure. We're located 30-60 minutes away from the airport, depending on whether you use a taxi or public transportation.
  • We will send you a link to complete the mandatory registration for all travelers. Please ensure that all guests in your reservation complete it before the check-in day.
  • Bed preferences for each bedroom: would you prefer one extra-large double bed or two single beds? Sofa bed?
  • We're passionate about hospitality and would like to provide you with a personalized experience that will make your stay unforgettable. Please let us know if we can do anything to make your stay even more enjoyable.
