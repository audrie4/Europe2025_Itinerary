Purcell Family Europe 2025 Trip Itinerary

Madrid Itinerary: 2 Days of Culture, Cuisine & Cocktails

Day 1: Iconic Landmarks & Sunset Cocktails

Morning

  • Breakfast at El Riojano
    Historic 19th-century bakery, renowned for rich hot chocolate and traditional pastries.
  • 10:00 Tuk Tuk City Tour
    2 hour eco-friendly tour of key landmarks: Royal Palace, Almudena Cathedral, and more.
    Booking Reference: https://www.getyourguide.com/en-gb/madrid-l46/welcome-tour-to-madrid-by-electric-tuk-tuk-t394363/

Midday

  • Explore Puerta del Sol
    Visit the bustling heart of Madrid: Kilometer Zero plaque & Bear and Strawberry Tree statue.
  • Stroll to Plaza Mayor
    Historic square known for stunning architecture and vibrant atmosphere.

Afternoon

  • Lunch at Casa Revuelta
    Famous for deep-fried salt cod fritters in a classic tavern setting.

Evening

  • Sunset Cocktails at La Nota Alta
    Rooftop bar with panoramic views of the Madrid skyline.
  • Dinner at Arrabal
    Contemporary Spanish cuisine in Plaza Mayor; try their award-winning “Best Cachopo in the World 2025.”

Day 2: Cultural Immersion & Gastronomic Experiences

Morning

  • Breakfast at Scarlett Café
    Trendy spot for pancakes and a cozy ambiance.
  • Visit El Retiro Park & Crystal Palace
    Explore gardens and the stunning glass pavilion.

Midday

  • Browse Mercado de San Miguel
    Sample gourmet tapas and local delicacies.
  • Visit Monasterio del Corpus Christi
    Buy handmade cookies from cloistered nuns via turntable system.
  • Explore Ikono Museum
    Interactive and immersive art installations.

Evening

  • 6:00 PM: Flamenco Show & Dinner at Corral de la Morería
    Authentic performance at Calle de la Morería, 17, 28005 Madrid.
    Website
  • 8:30 PM: Cocktails at Salmon Guru
    Innovative drinks at Calle de Echegaray, 21, 28014 Madrid.
    Website
    Open Sun–Thu 4 PM–2 AM; Fri–Sat 4 PM–2:30 AM. Walk-in only—arrive early.
