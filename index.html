Europe 2025: Travel Guide

🌍 Purcell Family’s 2025 European Trip – Buckle Up! ✈️

Welcome to the Purcell Family Europe 2025 Travel Guide, where luxury is non-existent, legroom is a myth, and James will undoubtedly upset his mum before we even board the plane. We’ll be squished down the back of the plane, be grumpy with the jet lag, and embarking on an adventure filled with culture, food, and the occasional travel mishap.

From Barcelona’s bustling streets and Madrid’s royal charm to Portugal’s stunning beaches in Lagos and Vale do Lobo, we’ll be soaking up culture, sunshine, and eat yummy food. In Portugal, we’ll meet friends along the way, adding to the chaos and fun. For the final week, we’ll be welcoming Jess happy face back to the family—excitement levels will be high!

Stay tuned as we keep updating the document while we keep updating the trip. Dad has been a great help!!
