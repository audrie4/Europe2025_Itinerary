Destination 1: Barcelona (30 June 2025, 3-4 nights)

Barcelona, the vibrant capital of Catalonia, is known for its stunning architecture (including Gaudí’s masterpieces), lively markets, and Mediterranean atmosphere.

  • Must-See Sights: Sagrada Família, Park Güell, Las Ramblas
  • Popular Neighborhoods: Gothic Quarter, Eixample, Barceloneta
  • Cuisine Highlights: Tapas, paella, fresh seafood
